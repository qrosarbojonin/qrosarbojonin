About Us

QUERETARO SARBOJONIN is brainchild of Indian community living in Queretaro and was established in 2022. However, Indian communities from other states of Mexico and Bangladesh communities in Mexico, have played a major role to conceptualize and implement.

The meaning of SARBOJONIN is OPEN TO ALL. Anyone from any part of the world, irrespective of their nationality, language, caste, creed, religion, gender, socio-economic status, can participate in the festivals arranged under this umbrella of “SARBOJONIN - Open to All”.

We are pretty sure that the platform of “SARBOJOIN” will help us the fraternity among humankind and bring much needed peace and equity across the globe..

In the first year (2022), we celebrated QUERETARO SARBOJONIN DURGOTSAB successfully in a grand scale, with the help of the unconditional volunteering with passion and dedication, spontaneous contributions in cash or kind from well-wishers. We could integrate the people through whole-day program of pooja (rituals and reading of scripts, etc.), Cultural Events, Mahabhog (Strictly vegetarian foods).

It’s worth mentioning that DURGOTSAB has been declared as INTANGIBLE CULTURAL HERITAGE OF HUMANITY by UNESCO in 2021.
