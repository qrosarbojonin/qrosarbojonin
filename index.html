QUERETARO SORBOJONIN DURGOTSAB

Queretaro Sarbojonin celebrating their first ever Durga Puja at Queretaro, Mexico.

About Us

This is for the first time that a Sarbojonin Durga Puja is being organized on this scale in Mexico, with an expected participation of 300 Indians and Mexicans. A representative puja of Latin America, the Queretaro Puja Committee shall celebrate the austere beauty of this world heritage event of Latin America, and place it up as its signature event on the international cultural map. Representatives from Gurudev Tagore Cultural Center of the Embassy of India shall attend the Puja, only a hundred miles away from the center of Mexico City proper, at an excellent venue in a jewel city of industries, investments and tourism in the context of the whole of Mexico and Latin America.
